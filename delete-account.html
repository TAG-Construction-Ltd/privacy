TAG Connect · Account Deletion

Delete your account

This page explains how to request deletion of your TAG Connect account and the personal data TAG Construction Limited holds about you.

Last updated: 18 June 2026

How to request deletion

TAG Connect is provided by TAG Construction Limited. You can request that your account and associated personal data be deleted in either of the following ways:

  1. By email. Send a request to our Data Protection Officer at support@tagconstructionltd.co.uk with the subject line "Account deletion request". Please include the full name and the phone number or email address registered to your TAG Connect account so we can verify your identity.
  2. In the app. Open TAG Connect and go to Settings → Delete my account, then follow the steps to request account deletion.

We will verify your identity before actioning any request, and will confirm once your account has been deleted. Requests are normally completed within 30 days.

What is deleted

When your deletion request is actioned, we remove the personal data associated with your account, including:

  • your name, contact details, date of birth, address, and National Insurance number
  • photos of identity documents (passport, driving licence)
  • qualification cards (CSCS, CPCS, NPORS)
  • work history and site diary entries
  • emergency contact details

What is kept, and for how long

Some data must be retained after account deletion where we are legally required to keep it. We retain only what the law requires, and securely delete it once the relevant period ends.

DataRetention periodReason
Financial and payment records 7 years HMRC requirement
Employment / engagement records Up to 6 years after your last engagement UK employment law

After these periods expire, the remaining data is securely deleted.
